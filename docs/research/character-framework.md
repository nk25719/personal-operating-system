# Character Development Framework

POS uses seven character-building tools:

1. Habituation through practice
2. Reflection on personal experience
3. Engagement with virtuous exemplars
4. Dialogue that increases virtue literacy
5. Awareness of situational variables and biases
6. Moral reminders
7. Friendships of mutual accountability

## Product Translation

- Habituation -> small repeated habits and streaks
- Reflection -> journal prompts and weekly reviews
- Exemplars -> role-model research and behavior modeling
- Dialogue -> AI coaching and scenario reflection
- Situational awareness -> environment review and bias prompts
- Moral reminders -> identity statements and values prompts
- Accountability -> trusted people, check-ins, and support loops
